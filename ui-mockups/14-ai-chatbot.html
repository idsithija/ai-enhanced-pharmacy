Can I take it with Ibuprofen?
🤖 AI Assistant
⚠️ Drug Interaction Warning:

Taking Aspirin and Ibuprofen together is generally NOT recommended as:
• Both are NSAIDs (Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs)
• Increased risk of stomach bleeding
• Reduced effectiveness of both medications

Recommendation: Consult your pharmacist or doctor before combining these medications.
